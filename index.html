About Eco-Explorer

An educational simulation game where players become Lead Ecologists in Brazil's Itatiaia National Park.

Eco-Explorer drops players into the heart of one of Brazil's most vital nature reserves. The mission is to explore, research, and understand the intricate web of life within the park.

Role-play as a Lead Ecologist driving research and conservation

Experience dynamic day/night cycles and weather systems

Unlock knowledge through research and exploration

Game Features

Core mechanics that make learning about ecology engaging and rewarding.

Explore & Discover

Venture into the park to find species with a dynamic encounter system influenced by weather and time.

Research & Learn

Observe animals to trigger educational quizzes and unlock knowledge about species.

Master & Progress

Reach mastery levels to unlock special abilities and progress through the ecosystem.

Our Vision

Creating a living, breathing ecosystem simulation that goes beyond traditional educational games.

Living World

Using AI and procedural generation to create a truly dynamic ecosystem where every interaction matters.

Knowledge-Based Progression

Learning about the ecosystem directly unlocks new areas and abilities, making education intrinsically rewarding.
