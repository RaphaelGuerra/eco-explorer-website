06. About Eco-Explorer

Eco-Explorer represents a revolutionary approach to educational gaming, shifting from a game about an ecosystem to a playable, living ecosystem. Players step into the role of Lead Ecologists in Brazil's Itatiaia National Park, tracking the elusive on√ßa pintada (jaguar) and discovering the intricate web of life within one of the country's most vital nature reserves.

The game combines cutting-edge AI technology with ecological science to create an immersive learning experience. Through dynamic weather systems, procedural content generation, and AI-powered interactions, players discover the wild beauty of the Brazilian rainforest while developing real conservation skills.

Here are the core technologies powering Eco-Explorer:

  • React & TypeScript
  • AI-Powered Interactions
  • Procedural Generation
  • Dynamic Weather
  • Agent-Based AI
  • Conservation Science
