The Game: Eco-Explorer

An educational simulation game where players become the Lead Ecologist of a vibrant, living ecosystem.

Eco-Explorer: Itatiaia Nature Sanctuary is a single-page React application that drops players into the heart of one of Brazil's most vital nature reserves. The core mission is to explore, research, and understand the intricate web of life within the park.

Role-play as a Lead Ecologist, driving research and conservation efforts.

Experience a dynamic world with day/night cycles and weather that impact gameplay.

Engage in a deep research progression system, unlocking skills and knowledge.

Uncover the secrets of the ecosystem through exploration and puzzle-like investigation.

The World: Itatiaia National Park

Explore a meticulously modeled digital twin of Brazil's first and oldest national park, a sanctuary of biodiversity.

A Legacy of Conservation

Established in 1937, the park is a critical remnant of the Atlantic Forest biome, showcasing incredible ecological diversity.

Two Worlds in One

From the lush, waterfall-filled forests of the Lower Itatiaia to the rugged, high-altitude plains of the Upper Itatiaia, the park offers distinct biomes to explore.

A Haven for Wildlife

Home to over 350 bird species, 102 primate species, and numerous threatened mammals. Every exploration is a chance for a rare encounter.

Core Gameplay Mechanics

A rewarding loop of exploration, research, and mastery that keeps players engaged and learning.

Explore & Encounter

Venture into the park to find species. A dynamic encounter system, influenced by weather and time, includes a 5% chance to find ultra-rare 'Radiant Variants' for massive XP bonuses.

Research & Master

Observe animals to trigger educational quizzes. Correct answers grant Research XP, leveling up your knowledge of a species. Reaching Level 5 unlocks permanent 'Symbiotic Perks'.

Symbiotic Perks

Mastering a species grants powerful abilities. Jaguar Mastery unlocks 'Night Vision' for exploring dark caves, while Capybara Mastery provides a 'Calming Presence,' increasing rare species encounters.

AI-Powered Eco-Log

Your digital field journal evolves with your research. Unlock AI-generated fun facts, anime-style avatars, and finally, stunning photorealistic portraits for each species you master.

Our Vision: A Living Sanctuary

We are moving beyond static games to create a playable, living ecosystem—a new paradigm in interactive education.

Living World Architecture

Using Procedural Content Generation (PCG) and Agent-Based AI, we simulate a world that feels truly alive. Animal populations follow ecological models (Lotka-Volterra), and individual agents make their own decisions, leading to emergent, unscripted behaviors.

Progression Through Knowledge

Our hybrid progression model combines traditional leveling with a 'Field Researcher' skill tree (Zoology, Botany, Ecology). Critically, knowledge itself is the key. Learning about the ecosystem directly unlocks new areas, tools, and interactions, intrinsically rewarding curiosity.

Emergent Narrative Engine

We don't write a story; we build a story generator. The narrative emerges from the dynamic interplay between the simulated ecosystem and the player's choices. Every action has a tangible, lasting impact, creating a unique chronicle for every player.

Future-Proof AI Systems

We leverage Generative AI to create dynamic 'micro-quests' based on the current state of the ecosystem. An AI 'Chaos Director' can introduce plausible long-term events like invasive species, ensuring the simulation is always unpredictable and challenging.

Endgame & Community

Fostering long-term engagement and connecting players to real-world conservation.

Capstone Projects

High-level players can undertake ambitious conservation projects like species reintroduction or watershed restoration, leaving a permanent mark on their simulated world.

Citizen Science Hub

Integrated systems for sharing discoveries, community-wide research goals, and a 'Scenario Editor' empower players to create and share their own challenges.

Real-World Impact

We will partner with conservation organizations active in Itatiaia. A portion of revenue from future expansions will be donated to support their vital on-the-ground work.
